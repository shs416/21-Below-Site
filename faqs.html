FAQs

What is a snowflake?

A snowflake is a dessert made with soft, creamy snow and often topped with fruit, ice cream, and other delicious treats. Our snow also comes in a variety of flavors such as Original and Thai Tea.

What is a cheese tea?

A cheese tea is a tea topped with sea salt cream cheese foam. It has a delicious creamy consistency and adds a unique flavor profile to the tea. It can be added to four types of tea: Jasmine Green Tea, Oolong Tea, Osmanthus Oolong Tea, and Peach Oolong Tea.

Do you have any dairy-free or vegan items?

Yes, all our fruit teas are made without dairy, eggs, or honey.

Do any of your products contain nuts?

Yes, our Korean Style, Oreo, and Thai Tea Snowflakes and our Korean Style Toast contain Almonds. However, these items can be made without Almonds upon request.

Can you adjust the sweetness in my tea?

We are more than happy to make tea to your liking. When ordering, please let us know what percentage of sweetness you would prefer.

Do you work with student organizations for fundraising or catering?

Yes! Please contact us for more information about our rates.

Is the store environmentally friendly?

Yes! All our Snowflakes and Toasts are served on glass plates and bowls. Additionally, our fruit teas are served in reusable cups. However, given the nature of our machinery, we are unable to use reuable cups for our milk-based teas.
